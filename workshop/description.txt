This mod overhauls the canning process in the game aimed to make it much more useful.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*]Adds a craftable [b]pressure canner[/b] to aid the canning process.
[*]Opening jars of food now returns an empty jar and a lid.
[*]Adjusted crafting requirements for the canning process to make it viable.
[*]Food in jars after preservation last for 1 year.
[/list]

[h1]FAQ:[/h1]

[h2]Does it work on multiplayer?[/h2]
Yes!
[h2]Does it work on existing saves?[/h2]
Yes! Existing canned foods will need to be rec-canned to last for 1 year.
[h2]Is it safe to remove the mod?[/h2]
Yes! You will [b]lose[/b] your pressure canner.
